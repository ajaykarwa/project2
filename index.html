The Shiva Vishnu Temple

Proudly serving Cleveland for over 30 years

ATTEND

Whether you're a lifelong Hindu, interested in learning more, or looking to meet the community, this is the place for you. With a full schedule of pujas, special events, and custom services, we have it all.

VOLUNTEER

Our volunteers are the engine that makes the temple run. From cooking lunch for hundreds of our members to providing health services to the elderly, our volunteers truly do it all. Lend us a helping hand today.

DONATE

Donations from our members help us to maintain our facilities and provide our community with the best services possible. Any and all contributions help push towards our goal!
